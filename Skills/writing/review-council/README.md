# Review Council

Convenes a panel of seven expert AI personas to review, stress-test, and improve any document, idea, proposal, or plan — then delivers a structured verdict with specific, actionable fixes.

This is not a proofreader. The Council exists to surface what the author can't see: blind spots, unstated assumptions, structural weaknesses, audience misalignment, and missed opportunities. It works on anything from a polished board deck to a half-formed idea spoken aloud.

## What you get

- **Seven distinct perspectives** — The Adversary, Audience Proxy, Architect, Analyst, Storyteller, Operator, and Strategist each review your content independently, then respond to each other by name
- **A structured verdict** — ASSESSMENT, RATING (Ready to Ship / Almost There / Needs Work / Rethink), CONFIDENCE score, TOP 3 ISSUES, TOP 3 STRENGTHS, and 5 CONCRETE FIXES in priority order
- **Content-type-aware scrutiny** — different personas lead for Word docs, Excel spreadsheets, PowerPoint decks, PDFs, and raw ideas
- **A MINORITY REPORT** — the strongest dissent that almost changed the outcome, so you know what you're overriding
